Top 5 Hero Manga/Manhwa You Should Read

4 Cut Hero

Genre: Comedy, Fantasy, Adventure

When the hero's journey is depicted in hilarious and unexpected ways, you get "4 Cut Hero." Follow the protagonist's adventures in a world where nothing is as it seems, and each page brings a new twist. This manga combines humor with fantasy elements, creating a unique and entertaining story that keeps readers on their toes.

Newbie Management

Genre: Action, Comedy, Isekai

"Newbie Management" masterfully blends action, comedy, and isekai elements to create a thrilling narrative. The protagonist, suddenly transported to a game-like world, must navigate a series of unexpected challenges while managing a team of rookie adventurers. As he grapples with his new reality, his wit and strategic thinking are put to the test. The story is filled with humorous moments and intense battles, offering a unique take on the classic hero's journey. The dynamic interactions between characters and the protagonist's growth keep readers engaged, while the vibrant artwork enhances the immersive experience. "Newbie Management" is a must-read for fans of adventurous comedies with a touch of fantasy.

My System is Serious

Genre: Action, Adventure, System

I will continuously fight to protect humanity, and for the well-being of all people! Constantly activating the restless system — definitely not because the locked enemy is the ‘Black Stocking Beast Girl.’

FFF Class Trashhero

Genre: Fantasy, Comedy, Action

"FFF Class Trashhero" is the tale of a hero who doesn’t fit the mold. Witness the protagonist's journey as they defy expectations, facing off against foes and allies alike in a world that demands more than just heroism. The series blends comedy with serious action, providing a fresh take on the hero genre.

Solo Leveling

Genre: Action, Fantasy, Adventure

"Solo Leveling" follows the weakest hunter, who rises to become the strongest. Dive into a world where dungeons and monsters are everyday threats, and one man stands against them all, defying his destiny. This manhwa is packed with action, intense battles, and a gripping storyline that captivates readers from start to finish.
